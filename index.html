About Me

Open to internship Open to collaboration

I am currently a PhD student at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Guoliang Fan. I obtained my B.Eng. (2022) at Wuhan University, advised by Prof. Wen Yang. Currently, I am jointly trained at Zhongguancun Academy in Beijing under the supervision of Prof. Chao Yu, and collaborating with Infinity-AI on developing and maintaining the RLinf framework.

Previously I was a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA), where I worked on multi-agent reinforcement learning under the guidance of mentor Xianliang Yang and Dr. Lei Song.

My current research interests include embodied intelligence, multi-agent reinforcement learning, and LLM for RL.

Research Focus
